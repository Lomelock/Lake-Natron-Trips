Volcano Sunrise Trek

Begin your adventure with a night ascent of Oldoinyo Lengai, the only active carbonatite volcano in the world and sacred to the Maasai. The trek is challenging but rewarding, culminating at the summit just as the sun rises. From the top, enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the Rift Valley and Lake Natron below.

  • Nighttime ascent for cooler temperatures
  • Stunning sunrise at the summit
  • Unique volcanic landscape
  • Cultural significance to the Maasai

Flamingo & Lake Tour

Explore the shores of Lake Natron, a unique soda lake famous for its large flocks of flamingos. The lake’s mineral-rich waters create striking salt flats that reflect the sky, making it a paradise for photographers and birdwatchers.

  • Salt flats and surreal landscapes

Footprints of the Past

Journey to the Laetoli area, where 3.6 million-year-old hominid footprints are preserved in volcanic ash. These ancient tracks provide a direct link to early human ancestors. A knowledgeable guide explains the scientific and historical significance of the site.

  • Visit to a world-famous archaeological site
  • Learn about human evolution
  • Guided interpretation of the footprints
  • Connection to Tanzania’s prehistoric heritage
