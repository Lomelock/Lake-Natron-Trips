Welcome to Natron Adventures

Natron Adventures is your gateway to experiencing the wonders of Lake Natron, guided by locals who know its secrets and stories. From the fiery slopes of Oldoinyo Lengai to the peaceful flamingo-studded shores, we take you beyond sightseeing—into a world of connection, culture, and discovery. Our tours are grounded in respect for nature and local communities, giving you a journey that's as meaningful as it is memorable.

Footprints of the Past

Explore the Laetoli area, home to the world-famous hominid footprints preserved in volcanic ash. These 3.6 million-year-old tracks offer a rare and direct connection to early human ancestors. A local guide explains the scientific and historical importance of these discoveries while you walk near this ancient site.
